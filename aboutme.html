About Me

Alison Luedke is an undergraduate student in The School of Journalism and Mass Communication at the University of Wisconsin-Madison. She is also enrolled in the Digital Studies certificate program and the Graphic Design certificate program. She holds an Associate of Science Degree from Madison College that was obtained in 2021. She works part time in the Specimen Processing Laboratory at Exact Sciences. She hopes to work within the fields of social media marketing and research after receiving her degree.
